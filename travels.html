AUSTRALIA 2024

SYDNEY

In January 2024 I travelled to the other side of the world to complete my PADI Divemaster qualificatioin with Dive Bondi. This took me 2 and a half months in Bondi, making friends, dving every day and becoming a local Aussie. I then flew up to Cairns and did 10 dives in the Great Barrier Reef! Visited the Atherton Tablelands and then flew down to Byron Bay. Went diving with Leopard sharks at Julian rocks and surfed at Byron Bay. Made lots of friends here with other travellers! Towards the end of March, I arrived back in Sydney to dive one last time with my dive buddies at Dive Centre Bondi before meeting my friend at the airport for our adventure together to New Zealand!

DIVE CENTRE BONDI

I am now a qualified PADI Divemaster! I really enjoyed my time at Dive Centre Bondi and miss all the people I met there. I made lots of friends, Philipp and Leon, two German boys on my course, Nell from California, Marley from the UK, and Cam and Eden from New Zealand to name a few! Everyone at this centre made my time so special and I feel very confident as a Divemaster because of them. I will definitely be back and stay in touch!

CAIRNS

I flew up to Cairns on the 11th of March 2024. Arrived and when on a liveaboard on the Great barrier Reef. As a diver this was a bucket list experience and saw the beautiul coral! After 3 days on boat, i visited the Atherton Tablelands and saw the Millaa Millaa waterfall, Lake Eacham, Josephine Falls and the Babinda Boulders.


BYRON BAY

After a week in Cairns, I flew down to the Gold Coast and caught a bus to Byron Bay. Surfer town with a hippy edge, and a beautiful Lighthouse walk, the diving here was incredible! I dove with Sundive Byron Bay and there were Leopard sharks everywhere at Julian Rocks. Most beautiful sharks I've seen. Made lots of friends here in my hostel, Raemma from Scotland, and Sophia and Katerin from Denmark! Felt like a sleepover everynight with these girls! Surfing Sun and Smiles!
